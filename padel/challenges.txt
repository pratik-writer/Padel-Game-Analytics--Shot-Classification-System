The first chalenge was detection of racket, which couldnot be handled with yolov8n
So had to switch to yolov8s, then racket detection was proper.

Earlier, the courts and people in the peripheral vision of camera i.e outside of the main focus court
were visible and skewed the shots count , which was fixed by defining
a court coordinates , focusing on only certain section that is highlighted by 
yellow color boundary in video.Rest of it was filtered.

Since ball is quite small and mostly in motion so -
- Tried this approach by making it a color tracking problem i.e
tracking the white color in motion as the rest of the court was static.
But it didnot work as while static it is a solid detectable color but 
in between the frames because of the motion blur and its small size, it was undetectable.

For shot classification firstly used arm swing methodology,
but it also classified normal hand moments on walking, moving etc as shots.
Then tried hybrid approach with the arm-wrist sangle, form the skeleton given by Mediapipe.
Relaxed threshold as it was getting to conscious regarding false positives that 
it didnot register some actual shots too.

The accuracy is low, and also i didnot train any additional models , just used the pre trained ones.
Training one for ball detection in frames would have given much better results.
